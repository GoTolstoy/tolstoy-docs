# Expense Guidelines

This document outlines Tolstoy's expense policies and reimbursement procedures.

## General Policy

All business expenses must be:
- Reasonable and necessary for business purposes
- Properly documented with receipts
- Submitted within 30 days of the expense

## Expense Categories

### Travel
- **Flights**: Economy class for domestic, business class for international flights over 6 hours
- **Hotels**: Up to standard room rate at approved hotels
- **Ground Transportation**: Rideshare, taxi, or rental car as appropriate

### Meals & Entertainment
- **Individual meals**: Reasonable cost during business travel
- **Team meals**: Prior approval required for groups over 5 people
- **Client entertainment**: Requires manager approval

### Office & Equipment
- **Home office**: One-time setup allowance for remote employees
- **Software**: Must be pre-approved by IT

## Submission Process

1. Collect all receipts
2. Submit expense report via the expense management system
3. Include business purpose for each expense
4. Manager approval required
5. Reimbursement processed within 2 weeks

## Questions

Contact the finance team for expense-related questions.
