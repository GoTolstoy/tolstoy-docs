# Budget Process

This document outlines Tolstoy's annual budget planning cycle.

## Annual Budget Cycle

### Q4 Planning (October - December)
1. **October**: Finance distributes budget templates
2. **November**: Department heads submit initial requests
3. **December**: Executive review and approval

### Ongoing Management
- Monthly variance analysis
- Quarterly forecast updates
- Mid-year budget reviews if needed

## Budget Categories

### Operating Expenses
- Personnel costs (salaries, benefits)
- Software and tools
- Marketing and sales
- General & administrative

### Capital Expenditures
- Equipment purchases
- Infrastructure investments
- Major software implementations

## Approval Thresholds

| Amount | Approval Required |
|--------|------------------|
| < $5,000 | Manager |
| $5,000 - $25,000 | Director |
| $25,000 - $100,000 | VP |
| > $100,000 | Executive Team |

## Budget Requests

For unplanned budget requests:
1. Complete the budget request form
2. Include business justification
3. Submit to your manager for initial review
4. Finance review and approval routing
