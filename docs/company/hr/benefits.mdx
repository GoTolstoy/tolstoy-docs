# Benefits Guide

Tolstoy offers a comprehensive benefits package to support our team.

## Health & Wellness

### Medical Insurance
- Multiple plan options available
- Company covers significant portion of premiums
- Coverage for employees and dependents

### Dental & Vision
- Dental insurance with preventive care coverage
- Vision insurance including eye exams and eyewear

### Mental Health
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Mental health coverage through medical plans
- Wellness resources and support

## Financial Benefits

### 401(k) Retirement Plan
- Company matching contributions
- Immediate vesting
- Wide selection of investment options

### Equity
- Stock option grants for eligible employees
- Competitive equity packages

### Life & Disability Insurance
- Company-paid life insurance
- Short-term and long-term disability coverage

## Work-Life Balance

### Flexible PTO
- Take time off when you need it
- No accrual caps or use-it-or-lose-it policies

### Parental Leave
- Generous paid parental leave
- Applies to all new parents

### Remote Work
- Flexible work location options
- Home office stipend

## Professional Development

### Learning Budget
- Annual learning and development allowance
- Conference attendance support
- Professional certification reimbursement

## Enrollment

Benefits enrollment occurs:
- Within 30 days of hire
- During annual open enrollment
- Upon qualifying life events
