# Company Policies

This section contains Tolstoy's workplace policies and guidelines.

## Employment Policies

### Equal Employment Opportunity
Tolstoy is committed to providing equal employment opportunities regardless of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

### At-Will Employment
Employment at Tolstoy is at-will, meaning either the employee or company may terminate the relationship at any time.

## Workplace Conduct

### Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to:
- Act with integrity and honesty
- Treat colleagues with respect
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

### Anti-Harassment Policy
Tolstoy maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment of any kind. Report concerns through appropriate channels.

## Time Off

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Flexible PTO policy for salaried employees
- Coordinate with your manager for planned absences
- Enter time off in the HR system

### Holidays
Company-observed holidays are published annually.

### Leave of Absence
Various leave options available including:
- Parental leave
- Medical leave
- Bereavement leave

## Remote Work

Tolstoy supports flexible work arrangements. Guidelines:
- Maintain regular communication with your team
- Be available during core hours
- Ensure secure home office setup
