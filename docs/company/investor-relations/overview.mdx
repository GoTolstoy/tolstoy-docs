# Investor Relations

The Investor Relations function manages relationships with investors and stakeholders.

## Key Activities

### Investor Communications
- Quarterly investor updates
- Annual reports
- Ad-hoc communications

### Fundraising Support
- Pitch materials
- Due diligence coordination
- Investor meetings

### Stakeholder Management
- Board relations
- Existing investor relations
- Potential investor pipeline

## Communication Guidelines

### Confidentiality
- Investor information is highly confidential
- Share only on need-to-know basis
- Follow disclosure policies

### Consistency
- All investor communications go through IR
- Consistent messaging across stakeholders
- Coordinate with leadership on key messages

## Contact

For investor-related inquiries, contact the Investor Relations team through appropriate channels.
