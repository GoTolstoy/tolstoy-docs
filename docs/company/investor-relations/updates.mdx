# Investor Updates

This section covers investor update processes and templates.

## Update Schedule

### Quarterly Updates
- Distributed within 30 days of quarter end
- Key metrics and highlights
- Product and business updates
- Financial summary

### Annual Reports
- Comprehensive year-in-review
- Strategic priorities for coming year
- Detailed financial information

## Update Content

### Standard Sections
1. **Executive Summary**: Key highlights and achievements
2. **Product Updates**: New features, launches, roadmap
3. **Business Metrics**: KPIs, growth metrics, milestones
4. **Financial Summary**: Revenue, burn, runway
5. **Team Updates**: Key hires, organizational changes
6. **Looking Ahead**: Priorities and focus areas

## Distribution

### Recipients
- Board members
- Major investors
- Strategic advisors

### Format
- Written memo format
- Supplementary data appendix
- Delivered via secure investor portal

## Confidentiality

All investor updates are confidential. Do not share outside approved distribution without explicit authorization.
