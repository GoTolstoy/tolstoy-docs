# Fundraising

Guidelines and resources for fundraising activities.

## Fundraising Process

### Preparation Phase
1. Define funding needs and use of proceeds
2. Prepare pitch materials
3. Update data room
4. Identify target investors

### Outreach Phase
1. Warm introductions preferred
2. Initial meetings and pitches
3. Follow-up conversations
4. Partner meetings

### Due Diligence Phase
1. Data room access
2. Reference calls
3. Technical/product deep dives
4. Financial analysis

### Closing Phase
1. Term sheet negotiation
2. Legal documentation
3. Final due diligence
4. Funding close

## Materials

### Pitch Deck
- Company overview and mission
- Problem and solution
- Market opportunity
- Product and traction
- Business model
- Team
- Financials and ask

### Data Room
- Corporate documents
- Financial statements
- Cap table
- Customer contracts (redacted)
- Employee information
- IP documentation

## Coordination

All fundraising activities must be coordinated through:
- CEO / Executive team
- Investor Relations
- Legal (for documentation)
- Finance (for financial materials)

## Confidentiality

Fundraising information is strictly confidential. Only discuss with authorized personnel.
