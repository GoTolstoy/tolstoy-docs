# Legal Department

The Legal department provides guidance on legal matters, contracts, and compliance.

## Key Areas

### Corporate Legal
- Corporate governance
- Board and stockholder matters
- Entity management

### Commercial Contracts
- Customer agreements
- Vendor contracts
- Partnership agreements

### Intellectual Property
- Trademark and patent management
- IP protection strategies
- Licensing

### Compliance & Privacy
- Regulatory compliance
- Data privacy (GDPR, CCPA)
- Security compliance

## When to Engage Legal

Contact the legal team for:
- Contract review and negotiation
- Legal questions or concerns
- Compliance inquiries
- IP matters
- Employment-related legal questions

## Request Process

1. Submit request through legal intake system
2. Legal team triages and assigns
3. Initial response within 2 business days
4. Complex matters may require additional time
