# Contract Resources

Guidelines for contract management at Tolstoy.

## Contract Types

### Customer Agreements
- Master Service Agreements (MSA)
- Order Forms
- Terms of Service
- Data Processing Agreements (DPA)

### Vendor Contracts
- Software licenses
- Service agreements
- Professional services
- NDAs

### Partnership Agreements
- Reseller agreements
- Integration partnerships
- Co-marketing agreements

## Contract Approval Process

### Standard Contracts
Using approved templates without modifications:
1. Complete the contract using approved template
2. Obtain business approval
3. Route for signature

### Non-Standard Contracts
Contracts with modifications or third-party paper:
1. Submit to legal for review
2. Legal reviews and provides feedback
3. Negotiate terms if needed
4. Final legal approval
5. Route for signature

## Approval Authority

| Contract Value | Annual | Approval Required |
|---------------|--------|-------------------|
| < $25,000 | Manager |
| $25,000 - $100,000 | Director |
| $100,000 - $500,000 | VP |
| > $500,000 | Executive + Legal |

## Contract Templates

Standard templates are available in the contract management system. Contact legal for access.

## Signature Authority

Only authorized signatories may sign contracts on behalf of Tolstoy. Check with legal for current authorized signers.
