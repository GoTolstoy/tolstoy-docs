# Compliance

Tolstoy is committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance.

## Data Privacy

### GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- Applies to EU/EEA data subjects
- Requires lawful basis for processing
- Data subject rights must be honored
- Data Processing Agreements required with processors

### CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- Applies to California residents
- Consumer rights to access, delete, and opt-out
- Privacy notice requirements

### Privacy Principles
- Collect only necessary data
- Use data only for stated purposes
- Implement appropriate security measures
- Retain data only as long as needed

## Security Compliance

### SOC 2
- Type II certification maintained
- Annual audits conducted
- Trust Service Criteria: Security, Availability, Confidentiality

### Data Security
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments

## Employee Responsibilities

All employees must:
- Complete required compliance training
- Report potential compliance issues
- Protect confidential information
- Follow data handling procedures

## Reporting Concerns

If you become aware of potential compliance issues:
1. Report to your manager or legal team
2. Use anonymous reporting channels if preferred
3. No retaliation for good-faith reports

## Training

Required compliance training:
- Annual security awareness training
- Data privacy training
- Code of conduct review
